First Roll of 120 Cinestill 800T

2017.03.13
Share
Tweet

It has been about a year since Cinestill Film successfully funded its 120 and 4x5 Cinestill 800T campaign on Indiegogo. Recently I received my pack (Finally!), in the past weekend I went Aberdeen Reservoir for a hike trip.

Although the films are still in the “alpha run”, the results are quite pleasing, with tons of details and the tone that I really like. It was a very foggy and dull day, and the colour turned out better than I expected!

All photos are taken with Fujica GW690II, with slight exposure and contrast adjustments. The light leak in the last shot was due to my forgetful mind; I didn’t lock the film in place after loading the film… :(

Next, I will probably shoot some sunlight and indoor shots. I still have 2 rolls of 120 and a box of 4x5 Cinestill 800T, that probably takes me a year or two to finish them all. I hope 120 format will be available to public soon! I want to shoot more!
